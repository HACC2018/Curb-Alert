WAYST

If there is waste, there is a WAY! Time doesn't stop, and that means waste doesn't either! WAYST is a web and mobile application made for you to track waste and help reduce it. Use WAYST to gain knowledge on how to handle your waste and how to reduce it.

Performing a waste audit? No problem! WAYST makes it easy to log large amounts of data with our easy-to-use online forms available on both desktop and mobile applications!
